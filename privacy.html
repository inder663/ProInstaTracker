Overview

Insta Tracker is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your data when you use our application.

  • We do not collect personal data.
  • All data processing occurs locally on your device.
  • Your exported Instagram JSON data remains private.

Information We Collect

Insta Tracker does not collect any personal information or data from its users. The app does not require login credentials and processes Instagram JSON data solely uploaded by the user.

How We Use Data

The app uses the uploaded Instagram JSON data to provide you with analytics on followers and account engagement. This information assists users in understanding their Instagram audience better.

Sharing

We do not sell or share your data with third parties. Your privacy is our priority, and we ensure that all uploaded data remains local and confidential.

Analytics

Insta Tracker does not utilize in-app analytics tools or services that collect user data. All processing is done directly with the uploaded JSON data.

Cookies/Tracking

Insta Tracker does not use cookies or tracking mechanisms. Our application is designed to function without any user tracking.

Data Retention

Since we do not collect or retain any user data, users are responsible for managing their uploaded Instagram JSON files within the app.

Security

We employ measures to secure the data you upload within the app. However, since the processing occurs on your device, it is crucial to ensure your device remains secure.

Children's Privacy

Insta Tracker does not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has used our app, please contact us.

International Users

Insta Tracker is available to users worldwide. By using this app, you consent to the transfer of your uploaded data as described in this policy.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, modify, and delete any data you have uploaded to the app. As we do not retain any data post-usage, users must manage their uploads.

Changes

We may update our Privacy Policy occasionally to reflect changes in our practices. We will notify users of any significant updates.
